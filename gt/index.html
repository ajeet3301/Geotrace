Capabilities

EVERYTHING YOU NEED

A complete geolocation toolkit powered by cutting-edge AI and open-source mapping technology.

🛰️

EXIF GPS EXTRACTION

Instantly reads hidden GPS coordinates embedded in photo metadata. Works with JPG, PNG, HEIC, and WEBP files from all major smartphones and cameras.

🤖

CLAUDE AI VISION

When GPS data is absent, Claude analyzes architecture, vegetation, road signs, license plates, language, sky angle, and cultural markers to predict location.

🗺️

INTERACTIVE MAPS

Visualize results on a beautiful Leaflet.js map powered by OpenStreetMap. No extra API keys needed — completely free with full zoom and pan controls.

📡

LIVE GPS TRACKING

Real-time location tracking with path history, speed readouts, heatmaps, and geofence alerts. Track yourself and your whole team on one map.

🔐

GOOGLE AUTH

Secure one-click sign-in via Firebase Authentication. Your Anthropic API key lives only in your browser — never stored on our servers.

⚙️

ADMIN DASHBOARD

Full admin panel with realtime analytics, user management, search monitoring and history. Complete control over your platform.

The Process

HOW IT WORKS

1

Upload Your Photo

Drag and drop or click to select any JPG, PNG, HEIC, or WEBP image from your device. Supports files up to 10MB.

2

EXIF Metadata Scan

GeoTrace instantly extracts EXIF metadata including GPS coordinates, camera info, and shooting settings from your photo.

3

AI Visual Analysis

If GPS data is missing, Claude AI analyzes every visual element — landmarks, text, vehicles, and environmental clues.

4

Pin on the Map

The predicted location is pinned on an interactive map with a confidence score and detailed reasoning from the AI.
