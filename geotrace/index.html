From raw GPS metadata to full AI visual analysis — GeoTrace covers every angle to identify exactly where your photos were taken.

EXIF GPS EXTRACTION

Instantly reads embedded GPS coordinates from your photo's EXIF metadata. Supports JPG, PNG, HEIC, and WebP — coordinates plotted in milliseconds.

CLAUDE AI VISION

When GPS data isn't available, Claude Vision AI analyzes landmarks, architecture, vegetation, signs, and terrain to predict location with precision.

PROCESS

HOW GEOTRACE
WORKS

01

UPLOAD YOUR PHOTO

Drag and drop any image — JPG, PNG, HEIC, WebP. Your file is processed entirely in your browser and never uploaded to our servers.

02

EXIF DATA EXTRACTION

We instantly scan your photo's EXIF metadata — GPS coordinates, camera model, timestamp, focal length, and more, displayed in a clean table.

03

AI VISUAL ANALYSIS

Claude Vision AI examines the image for landmarks, architecture style, vegetation, road signs, sky angle, and cultural markers to predict location.

04

SATELLITE PIN

The result flies cinematically to the location on a satellite map with a glowing neon crosshair marker and confidence radius ring.
