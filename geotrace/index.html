FEATURES

EVERYTHING YOU NEED

A complete geolocation suite — from single photo analysis to real-time team tracking.

🛰
EXIF GPS Extraction
Instantly reads GPS coordinates, camera model, aperture, ISO, shutter speed and more from photo metadata.
🤖
Claude AI Vision
When EXIF GPS is absent, Claude analyzes architecture, landscapes, vehicles and cultural markers to estimate location.
📡
Live GPS Tracking
Real-time location tracking with path history, speed, heading, and accuracy data. See all online users on one map.
🔥
Heatmap Analytics
Visualize your movement patterns as a heatmap. See where you spend the most time at a glance.
🔒
Geofence Alerts
Draw virtual boundaries on the map. Get instant alerts when you enter or exit a geofenced area.
📊
Route Playback
Replay your entire route on the map with animated playback. Analyze distance, duration, and speed graphs.
