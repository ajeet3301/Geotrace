Capabilities

EVERYTHING YOU NEED

A complete geolocation toolkit powered by cutting-edge AI and open-source mapping technology.

🛰️

EXIF GPS EXTRACTION

Instantly reads hidden GPS coordinates embedded in photo metadata. Works with JPG, PNG, HEIC, and WEBP files from all major smartphones and cameras.

🤖

CLAUDE AI VISION

When GPS data is absent, Claude analyzes architecture, vegetation, road signs, license plates, language, sky angle, and cultural markers to predict the location.

The Process

HOW IT WORKS

1

Upload Your Photo

Drag and drop or click to select any JPG, PNG, HEIC, or WEBP image from your device. Supports files up to 10MB.

2

EXIF Metadata Scan

GeoTrace instantly extracts EXIF metadata including GPS coordinates, camera info, and shooting settings from your photo.

3

AI Visual Analysis

If GPS data is missing, Claude AI analyzes every visual element in the image — landmarks, text, vehicles, and environmental clues.

4

Pin on the Map

The predicted location is pinned on an interactive map with a confidence score and detailed reasoning from the AI.
